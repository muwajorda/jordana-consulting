About Me

I am passionate about advancing precision medicine, with a special focus on health equity. My work aims to bridge the gap in healthcare by ensuring that underserved populations, especially Black patients, have access to personalized therapy options. My current project involves analyzing TCGA data to investigate racial disparities in therapy responses for breast, prostate, and ovarian cancers. The goal is to create a product that empowers doctors with better tools for informed decisions and offers Black patients more tailored treatment options.

My Current Project

Exploring Racial Disparities in Cancer Treatment

My focus is on analyzing TCGA data from Black patients to identify biomarkers in breast, prostate, and ovarian cancers that can lead to better therapy. This data-driven investigation aims to uncover key insights that will help healthcare professionals develop personalized treatment plans, ensuring equity in medical outcomes.

The end result of this research could potentially evolve into a product that provides doctors with a platform offering more informed decisions for treating Black patients.
